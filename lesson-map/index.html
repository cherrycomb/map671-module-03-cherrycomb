Tennessee Waterways

Authored by: RL Martens aka cherrycomb

This map shows the major waterways of Tennessee. It was made with QGIS-LTR software on MacOS. The data is from the National Atlas of the United States, 1:1,000,000-scale datasets. March 2016, 2nd Edition. The National Map Small-Scale website. The map uses CRS NAD83.
